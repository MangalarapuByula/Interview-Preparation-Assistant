Interview Tips

Before the Interview

  • Research the company and role thoroughly.
  • Practice common interview questions.
  • Dress appropriately for the interview.
  • Prepare questions to ask the interviewer.

During the Interview

  • Listen carefully and answer questions clearly.
  • Show enthusiasm and confidence.
  • Ask for clarification if you don't understand a question.
  • Highlight relevant skills and experiences.

After the Interview

  • Send a thank-you email or note to the interviewer.
  • Reflect on your performance and areas for improvement.
  • Follow up if you haven't heard back within the expected timeframe.
