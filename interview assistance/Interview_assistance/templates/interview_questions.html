Interview Questions Repository

Computer Science/Information Technology:

  • Explain the difference between abstraction and encapsulation.
  • What is the difference between a thread and a process?
  • Explain the concept of polymorphism with an example.
  • What is a database index, and how does it improve performance?
  • Describe the OSI model and its layers.
  • Explain the difference between HTTP and HTTPS.
  • What is SQL injection, and how can it be prevented?
  • How does a CDN (Content Delivery Network) work?
  • What are the advantages of using version control systems like Git?
  • Explain the working principle of a TCP/IP protocol.

Electrical Engineering:

  • What is the difference between AC and DC?
  • Describe the working principle of a transformer.
  • What are the different types of motors, and where are they used?
  • Explain the concept of power factor correction.
  • What is the purpose of a relay in an electrical circuit?
  • Describe the operation of a solar PV (photovoltaic) system.
  • How does a capacitor store energy?
  • What is the difference between a fuse and a circuit breaker?
  • Explain the concept of three-phase power.
  • How does a microcontroller differ from a microprocessor?

Mechanical Engineering:

  • Explain the difference between stress and strain.
  • Describe the working principle of an internal combustion engine.
  • What are the different types of manufacturing processes?
  • What is the purpose of a heat exchanger?
  • Explain the laws of thermodynamics.
  • What are the advantages and disadvantages of using a CNC machine?
  • How does a hydraulic system work?
  • Describe the operation of a refrigeration cycle.
  • What are the different types of materials used in engineering applications?
  • Explain the working principle of a centrifugal pump.

Civil Engineering:

  • Describe the difference between shallow and deep foundations.
  • What are the properties of good building materials?
  • Explain the process of soil compaction.
  • What are the different types of bridges, and how do they differ?
  • Describe the steps involved in a construction project.
  • Explain the concept of reinforced concrete.
  • What is the significance of surveying in civil engineering?
  • Describe the purpose of a retaining wall.
  • How does a water treatment plant work?
  • What are the factors to consider in highway design?
